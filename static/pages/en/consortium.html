Our partners

Enhancing Collaboration and Visibility Partners collectively aim to enhance the visibility and functionality of DCA services across Europe. By focusing on communication enhancements and creating a unified approach to dual career support, Athlete Connect project is poised to foster a more cohesive, supportive, and accessible environment for athletes across Europe, promoting sustainable practices and innovative solutions in the realm of dual careers.

Fondation du Sport Francais (FSF)

About

Fondation du Sport Francais is a key player in France’s sports ecosystem, focusing on promoting sports and supporting athletes through various initiatives, including corporate patronage.

Strategic Aim:

FSF aims to broaden the scope and accessibility of support through corporate patronage by creating a detailed database of athletes to facilitate better patronage relationships and to serve as a central reference within France for athlete support.

https://www.fondation-du-sport-francais.fr

ASD Margherita Sport e Vita (MSV)

About

ASD Margherita Sport e Vita is an Italian sports association with a strong focus on basketball. It is known for its commitment to supporting elite athletes, especially in transitioning from active sports careers to new professional endeavours.

Strategic Aim:

MSV plans to use AthleteConnect’s digital toolbox to refine its strategies for integrating sports with educational pathways and to provide enhanced mentoring for career transitions post-sport.

https://www.msvbasket.it

ZMSD Branik

About

ZMSD Branik is a major sports organization based in Maribor, Slovenia, with strong connections to the Slovenian Olympic Committee and various national sports governing bodies. It has been involved in extensive human resource development programs targeting various stakeholders in the sports domain.

Strategic Aim:

The project aims to enhance communication and operational connections with local sports clubs and educational institutions, increasing the effectiveness and reach of dual career programs, and addressing communication shortcomings of past initiatives.

https://www.szm.si/zveza-mariborskih-športnih-društev-branik

University Sport Association of Poland (AZS)

About

University Sport Association of Poland is one of the largest sports associations in Poland, with a membership exceeding 25,000. It plays a crucial role in promoting sports within academic settings across Poland and has been actively involved in research and development of sports education programs.

Strategic Aim:

AZS seeks to utilise AthleteConnect to standardise and enhance dual career supports across Poland, improving program accessibility and effectiveness through the exchange of best practices and the development of new, more effective dual career programs.

https://azs.upwr.edu.pl/en/
