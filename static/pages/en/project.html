Empowering Athletes for Successful Dual Careers

About AthleteConnect

AthleteConnect (ACON) is at the forefront of transforming dual career pathways for athletes across Europe. By leveraging extensive research and successful initiatives from past projects, AthleteConnect develops state-of-the-art digital tools designed to enhance the visibility and accessibility of career counselling and dual career services.

Be connected – Stay active!

We strive to establish and expand robust networks that are not only visible but also easily accessible, ensuring that every athlete has the support they need to succeed both in and out of the sporting arena.

Our Mission

Enhance the quality and capacity of sport and public organisations

Our mission is to enhance the quality and capacity of sport and public organisations by enabling effective transnational and cross-sector collaboration. AthleteConnect boosts the resilience and competitiveness of stakeholders within the dual career domain by expanding their digital capabilities, ensuring they are well-equipped to meet the challenges of today’s dynamic sports industry.
